# Executive Summary

This SQL project analyzed Olist marketplace performance using cleaned gold-layer tables in DuckDB. The analysis focused on revenue, customer geography, seller performance, product category demand, and delivery efficiency.

The results show that São Paulo (SP) is the dominant marketplace region on both the demand and supply sides. Product revenue is distributed across multiple categories, with home-related products emerging as strong drivers. Seller performance is highly uneven, with some sellers generating strong revenue and maintaining excellent delivery performance, while others contribute disproportionately to late deliveries.

Overall delivery performance is acceptable, with approximately 92% of delivered orders arriving on time. However, seller-level analysis shows that delays are concentrated among specific underperforming sellers rather than evenly distributed across all regions or sellers.

This suggests that targeted seller performance improvement could have a meaningful impact on overall customer experience.
