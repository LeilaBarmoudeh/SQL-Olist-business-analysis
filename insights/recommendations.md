# Recommendations

## 1. Monitor Seller Delivery KPIs
Track seller late delivery rate alongside revenue and order volume. Revenue alone is not enough to measure seller quality.

## 2. Prioritize Underperforming Sellers
Investigate sellers with delay rates above marketplace average, especially those with sufficient order volume to indicate persistent issues.

## 3. Use Top Sellers as Operational Benchmarks
Study the processes of top-performing low-delay sellers and apply those standards to weaker sellers.

## 4. Focus on High-Impact Regions
Since São Paulo (SP) and Rio de Janeiro (RJ) contribute a large share of delayed orders or high delay rates, operational improvements there can create a larger business impact.

## 5. Continue Growing High-Performing Categories
Support strong product categories while also distinguishing between volume-driven and value-driven category strategies.

## 6. Be Careful with Small Samples
Use minimum-order thresholds when evaluating delivery performance by state or seller to avoid misleading conclusions.
